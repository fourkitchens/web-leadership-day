Web Leadership Day

Let’s bring the web together.

June 21, 2013 in Austin, TX

Calling all agencies, shops, and startups

Let’s all come together and learn from each other. Austin is a hot-bed for tech innovation, and the only way our thriving community will continue to grow is to come together to network, share, and learn from each other.

We’re calling on all Austin-based tech companies to come together for a day of curated talks, lightning demos, and Earth-shattering enlightenment*.

(*Earth-shattering enlightenment not guaranteed)

Selected talks on curated topics

Come and learn about:

  • Alternatives to RFPs for business
  • Explaining the importance of UX to clients
  • Growing unique company cultures
  • Agile for agencies
  • Business development secrets

Lightning talks

Don’t see a topic you’d like to hear about? No worries, the afternoon brings us lightning talks. Sign up to talk about what is important to you and your business.

Want to demo a new product, tool, or idea you’ve been working on? This is a great place to do so!

Coffee talk

Is there a topic you’d like to explore further? Grab a table and host a break off session with industry peers.

Healthy competition at Tech Trivia Night

End the day proving you’re worth your salt at Tech Trivia Night! But we won’t test your tech knowledge, in this trivia quiz, nerd street cred will take you far.

We’re not in Austin because we have to be, we’re in Austin because we want to be. Let’s make the web a better place, starting right here in Austin.
